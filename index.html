About the BIT

The Business Information Technology Department prepares students with both IT and business skills to meet the modern market needs.

Our Vision

To be a pioneer academic department that meets modern market needs and keeps pace with the evolution of information technology.

Our Mission

Equip students with practical IT and business skills, enabling them to manage systems and contribute effectively in any organization.

Our Goal

Prepare students to solve real-world problems, stay updated with IT developments, work ethically, and collaborate effectively in teams.
